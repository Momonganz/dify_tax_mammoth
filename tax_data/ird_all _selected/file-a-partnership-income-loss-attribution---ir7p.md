Before you start
----------------

You will need:

*   the partner’s name and IRD number
*   details of any income/losses distributed to each partner and their proportion of profits/losses
*   details of any deductions for extinguished losses
*   details of the overseas tax paid, imputation credits and other tax credits allocated to each partner.

In myIR, go to your return and follow the prompts
-------------------------------------------------

*   Select your income types.
*   Enter the required information.
*   Complete any additional forms that apply to your situation.

Other ways to do this

You can also file your IR7P on paper.

1.  Download and fill in the IR7P.
2.  Print the completed IR7P, and sign it.
3.  Make a copy and attach the original to your income tax return for partnerships and look-through companies - IR7.
4.  Post the IR7, IR7P and any other partnership return documents to us:
    
    Inland Revenue  
    PO Box 39090  
    Wellington Mail Centre  
    Lower Hutt 5045
