Budget 2007 tax announcements
=============================

The business tax reform package announced in Budget 2007 ([$3.4 billion for investment and innovation](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-01-media-release-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055717&hash=B69C8981394447D9ABE5DB2842BDA334)
) includes:

*   **A cut in the company tax rate to 30%**, with effect from 2008-09. See:
    *   [Company tax rate change: questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-02-company-tax-rate-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055718&hash=C52329C98DF0D3AB8196CC81838B8D05)
        .
*   **A 15% tax credit for R&D.** See:
    *   [R&D fact sheet](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-03-randd-fact-sheet-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055719&hash=CA4211460A7F2E8D89E1232C96B8FAE3)
        
    *   [Questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-04-randd-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055720&hash=C9B66C77E0C5F17FD34C0A37648B4836)
        .
*   **A reduced, 30% tax rate for certain savings vehicles.** See:
    *   [Savers' fact sheet](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-05-savers-fact-sheet-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055721&hash=9FF0A2E24951B11077D802022E3ADA07)
        
    *   [Questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-06-savers-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055722&hash=59DC36058A3CC137F79A2080E5D4CEEF)
        .
*   **An active income exemption for New Zealand-controlled foreign companies.** See:
    *   [International tax fact sheet](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-07-international-tax-fact-sheet-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055723&hash=23DDD8A6D42B6EF18A7E7AC690B26EF7)
        
    *   [Questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-08-international-tax-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055724&hash=BEBE0B0F5B886D8EB3A7B1F9C187C9B5)
        
    *   [New Zealand's International Tax Review: an Update](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-09-international-tax-review-update-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055725&hash=5741DC90F7F1B8F5C6C3EEF8DAA19201)
        

Major KiwiSaver reforms ([Budget gives $40 a week to support savings](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-10-kiwisaver-support-for-savings-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055726&hash=7FDD70618040E6FBA84A071CCEEA32EE)
) include:

*   **A new members' tax credit** that matches their contributions up to $20 a week. See:
    *   [Savers – Fact sheet and Questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-11-kiwisaver-savers-fact-sheet-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055727&hash=74EE66DEB5E0EF1EFC22EF3396BEFDD4)
        
*   **Compulsory matching employer contributions**, to be phased in over four years, and **a new tax credit** to reimburse employers for the cost of matching contributions, capped at $20 a week. See:
    *   [Employers – Fact sheet and Questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-12-kiwisaver-employers-fact-sheet-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055729&hash=B7CD64224F32146FEEABFFA33B0D8143)

Other tax measures include:

*   **Tax incentives to boost charitable giving.** See:
    *   [Fostering a culture of charitable giving, Fact sheet – charitable giving and Investing in a culture of charitable giving: questions and answers](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-16-charitable-giving-fact-sheet-qa-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055733&hash=A6BACA809E105348172717F8F4FC3F43)
        
*   **Increased funding for Inland Revenue to strengthen auditing of property transactions.** See:
    *   [Tougher action on property speculation](/-/media/project/ir/tp/news/2007/2007-05-17-budget-2007-tax-announcements/2007-05-17-budget-2007-17-property-auditing-funding-pdf.pdf?sc_lang=en&modified=20200910055734&hash=16EB93A1C54EE88C7F9A2994B1B1705B)
