Annual Report 2021
==================

This is the Annual Report 2021 for Inland Revenue

Ko tēnei te Pūrongo ā-Tau 2021 a Te Tari Taake

Inland Revenue plays a critical role in improving the economic and social wellbeing of New Zealanders We collect over 80% of the Crown’s revenue to help fund government services for the people of New Zealand. We work to make it easier for New Zealanders to receive the support they need.
