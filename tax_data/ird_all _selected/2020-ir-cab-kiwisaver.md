Early access to KiwiSaver for people with life-shortening congenital conditions
===============================================================================

Information release for proposals that enable a person with a life-shortening congenital condition to withdraw their KiwiSaver savings early. 

The proposals were [announced on 21 July 2019](https://www.beehive.govt.nz/release/kiwisaver-changes-provide-savings-access-people-shortened-life-expectancy)
 and Supplementary Order Paper No 293 to the [Taxation (KiwiSaver, Student Loans, and Remedial Matters) Bill](/bills/52-158)
 was released on 13 August 2019.
