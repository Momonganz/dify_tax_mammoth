Ngā tauanga mō te whakatārewa penapena a ngā mema KiwiSaver Statistics on KiwiSaver members on savings suspensions
==================================================================================================================

All current KiwiSaver data, including monthly data, is available in Excel downloads located on the Datasets for KiwiSaver statistics page. Annual data is due to be uploaded by 30 September.

[Datasets for KiwiSaver statistics](/about-us/tax-statistics/kiwisaver/datasets)

Number of savings suspensions by type each month
------------------------------------------------

This graph shows the number of savings suspensions by type, over the past 12 months.

![Graph showing savings suspensions as per figure below](/-/media/project/ir/home/graphics/about-us/tax-statistics/kiwisaver/contributions/savings-suspensions/monthly_savings_suspensions_by_type-nov24.png?modified=20241217004401)

In November 2024:

*   87,726 KiwiSaver members were on a savings suspension, down from 95,276 in November 2023

Of this number:

*   1,312 KiwiSaver members were on financial hardship suspensions, down from 1,716 in November 2023
*   86,414 KiwiSaver members were on ordinary savings suspensions, down from 93,560 in November 2023.

Notes:

*   Active members can request savings suspensions. There are 2 types of savings suspension:
    *   ordinary - can be granted to any member after 12 months of membership
    *   financial hardship - can be granted earlier for members in financial hardship.

Number of savings suspensions by duration each month
----------------------------------------------------

This graph shows the number of KiwiSaver members on a savings suspension, by duration.

![Graph showing savings suspensions as per figure below](/-/media/project/ir/home/graphics/about-us/tax-statistics/kiwisaver/contributions/savings-suspensions/monthly_savings_suspensions_by_duration-nov24.png?modified=20241217004241)

In November 2024:

*   87,726 KiwiSaver members were on savings suspensions, down from 95,276 in November 2023
*   10,876 members were on savings suspensions from 3 to 11 months, up from 10,014 in November 2023
*   76,850 members were on savings suspensions for 12 months, down from 77,749 in November 2023
*   No members were on savings suspensions for 13 months to 5 years, compared to 7,513 in November 2023.

Notes:

*   Only active members can request savings suspensions.
*   There are 2 types of savings suspensions:
    *   ordinary - can be granted to any member after 12 months of membership
    *   financial hardship - can be granted earlier for members in financial hardship.
*   From 1 April 2019 an employee can apply for a savings suspension for a period from 3 months to 1 year. Previously, the period allowed was anywhere from 3 months to 5 years.
*   A KiwiSaver member can apply for a new savings suspension at any time, they do not need to wait for their current suspension to expire.
