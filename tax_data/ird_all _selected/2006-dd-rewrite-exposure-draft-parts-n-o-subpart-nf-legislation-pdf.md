Application (2) The NRWT rules apply to a person who makes a payment that consists of non- resident passive income. When agent or other person receives amount (2) Subsection (3) applies if— (a) an agent or other person in New Zealand receives a payment of non- resident passive income on behalf of a person entitled to the payment; and (b) some or all of the amount of tax for the payment has not been withheld. Obligation to withhold: agent or other person (3) The agent or other person must withhold the amount of tax referred to in subsection (2)(b) for the payment and pay it to the Commissioner. The obligation to withhold arises at the time of receipt.
