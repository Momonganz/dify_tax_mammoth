Ngā tatauranga o te tokomaha o ngā mema KiwiSaver e ai ki te moniwhiwhi Statistics on the number of KiwiSaver members by income
===============================================================================================================================

All current KiwiSaver data, including monthly data, is available in Excel downloads located on the Datasets for KiwiSaver statistics page. Annual data is due to be uploaded by 30 September.

[Datasets for KiwiSaver statistics](/about-us/tax-statistics/kiwisaver/datasets)

Number of KiwiSaver members, by annual income as at 30 June
-----------------------------------------------------------

This graph shows the number of active/provisional KiwiSaver members, by annual income as at 30 June 2024.

![](/-/media/project/ir/home/graphics/about-us/tax-statistics/kiwisaver/joining/member-demographics/number-of-kiwisaver-members-by-annual-income-2024.png?modified=20240916013609)

Between June 2014 and June 2024, the number of members who earned:

*   between $1 to $20,000 increased from 576,700 to 999,201
*   between $20,001 to $40,000 increased from 444,045 to 536,436
*   between $40,001 to $60,000 increased from 381,377 to 522,106
*   between $60,001 to $80,000 increased from 197,620 to 490,502
*   between $80,001 to $100,000 increased from 83,175 to 302,372
*   between $100,001 to $120,000 increased from 38,980 to 166,426
*   more than $120,000 increased from 67,832 to 290,520.

Notes:

*   All members as at 30 June 2024 with income for the 2023 tax year. Members with no income for the year are not included.
*   KiwiSaver membership includes people who have joined KiwiSaver and have since moved overseas, and members who are deceased but their account is still open.
*   From April 2020 KiwiSaver information is reported from a new system.
*   See data download for more details.
