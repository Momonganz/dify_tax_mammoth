If the volume of business in the country of residence is insignificant in comparison with the activities in Japan in terms of the value of assets or amount of income, or the contribution of the activity in the country of residence is negligible in the contribution of the total business, then this condition is not satisfied.

16 “Determination by the NTA Commissioner” in D of 3 means a determination by Article 1, item 1 of the Ministerial Ordinance for Determination under the Convention pursuant to the Act on Special Provisions of the Income Tax Act, the Corporation Tax Act and the Local Tax Act regarding the Application of Conventions.
