## View GST filing status

You can view the status of your current and past 2 years’ GST returns via  [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ "myTax Portal").

1. Log in to  [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ "myTax Portal").
    You must be assigned the role of either ‘Preparer’ or ‘Approver’ for _GST (Filing and Applications)_ in Corppass by the GST-registered business.
2. At “GST”, select “View GST Filing Status” and enter the accounting period you wish to view.

The status of your GST return(s) will be displayed.

- If the status shows "REQUIRED TO FILE ", you may click on the “E-FILE” button to retrieve the return.
- If the status shows “PROCESSED”, your return has been successfully submitted to IRAS.
