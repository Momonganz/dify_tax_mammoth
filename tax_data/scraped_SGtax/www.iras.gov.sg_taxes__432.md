You can view or print copies of the acknowledgement pages to your filed GST returns and other notices issued to you within the past 3 years via [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ "myTax Portal").

1. Log in to [myTax Portal](https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ "myTax Portal"). You
    must be assigned the role of either ‘Preparer’ or ‘Approver’ for _GST (Filing and Applications)_ in Corppass by the GST-registered business.
2. Go to “Notices/Letters” and select “GST”.
3. Click on the document name to view or download the notice/acknowledgement page.

_\*Please disable all pop-up blockers._

If you closed your browser before saving a copy of the acknowledgement page to your filed GST return, a softcopy of this page will be made available via the above steps **after 3 working days**.
