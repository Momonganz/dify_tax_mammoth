Where the supply qualifies as an [international service](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/goods-services-tax-(gst)/charging-gst-(output-tax)/when-to-charge-0-gst-(zero-rate)/providing-international-services) under Section 21(3) of the GST Act,
you may zero-rate the supply of service (i.e. charge GST at 0%).

On this page:

## Sale & lease of air & sea containers

[**Qualifying criteria to zero-rate**](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/goods-services-tax-(gst)/specific-business-sectors/air-and-sea-containers#qualifying-criteria-to-zero-rate)

The containers:

1. are used or to be used for international transportation of goods; and
2. comply with the prescribed requirements in the Eighth Schedule of GST (International Services) Order.

Please refer to paragraph (v) in the [extract of Section 21 of the GST Act](https://www.iras.gov.sg/media/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/list-of-international-services-extract.pdf?sfvrsn=d4781b9c_29 "list of international services extract") (PDF, 203KB).

#### Relevant section for zero-rating

S21(3)(v) of the GST Act

## Certain services (e.g. repair and maintenance)

Certain services comprising the **repair**, **maintenance** or **management** of any air or sea containers.

[**Qualifying criteria to zero-rate**](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/goods-services-tax-(gst)/specific-business-sectors/air-and-sea-containers#qualifying-criteria-to-zero-rate)

The containers:

1. are used or to be used for international transportation of goods; and
2. comply with the prescribed requirements in the Eighth Schedule of GST (International Services) Order.

Please refer to paragraph (w) in the [extract of Section 21 of the GST Act](https://www.iras.gov.sg/media/docs/default-source/uploadedfiles/pdf/list-of-international-services-extract.pdf?sfvrsn=d4781b9c_29 "list of international services extract")(PDF, 203KB).

#### Relevant section for zero-rating

S21(3)(w) of the GST Act
