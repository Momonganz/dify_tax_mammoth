Under MFT Scheme, approved businesses need not pay GST when making local purchases of approved marine fuel oil from any GST-registered suppliers.

## Purpose

This scheme is designed to benefit qualifying **businesses in the bunkering industry** that make local purchases of approved marine fuel oil ("MFO").

GST registered businesses approved under the Approved Marine Fuel Trader (MFT) Scheme need not pay GST on local purchases of approved marine fuel oil (e.g. MFO 380cst) from any GST-registered suppliers. This eases the cash flow difficulties of the approved businesses by eliminating the need to pay GST upfront and to subsequently claim it back by obtaining a refund from IRAS.

## Qualifying for the scheme

To qualify for the scheme, you should:

1. Be a business incorporated or registered in Singapore and your primary business activity is supplying bunker fuel for use onboard ocean-going ships;
2. Be registered for GST and have good compliance records in terms of tax compliance and payment;
3. Hold a valid bunkering licence with the Maritime Port Authority of Singapore (MPA); and
4. Have good internal controls and maintain high standards of accounting and record-keeping.

The Comptroller may exercise his direction discretion to vary the requirements or impose additional requirements. For more information, please refer to [Approved Marine Fuel Trader (AMFT) Scheme](https://www.iras.gov.sg/media/docs/default-source/e-tax/etxguide_gst_amft-scheme_5th_edition.pdf?sfvrsn=f9ae8444_14)  (PDF, 3980KB).

## Applying for the scheme

To apply, please submit

1. [GST F16: Application for Approved Marine Fuel Traders Scheme](https://go.gov.sg/f16amft "GST F16: Application for Approved Marine Fuel Traders Scheme"); and
2. The following documents to Comptroller of GST:
1. A concise description of the nature of your business (specify your main products, major suppliers and buyers of Marine Fuel Oil);
2. A copy of your bunker licence (valid for at least the next 3 months); and
3. A copy of your latest year-end audited financial statements.
