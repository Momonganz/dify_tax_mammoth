# Claiming Reliefs

[![Non-resident Individuals](https://www.iras.gov.sg/images/default-source/illustrations-png/non-resident-individuals.png?sfvrsn=51236092_3)\\
**Foreign Tax Credit** \\
\\
Companies may claim foreign tax credit for tax paid in a foreign jurisdiction against the Singapore tax payable on the same income.](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/corporate-income-tax/income-deductions-for-companies/claiming-reliefs/foreign-tax-credit)[![Commission-paying Organisations](https://www.iras.gov.sg/images/default-source/illustrations-png/commission--paying-organisations.png?sfvrsn=bed1b49d_3)\\
**Group Relief** \\
\\
Group Relief enables companies to deduct unutilised capital allowances/ trade losses/ donations of 1 company from the assessable income of another company in the same group.](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/corporate-income-tax/income-deductions-for-companies/claiming-reliefs/group-relief)[![Paying Stamp Duty](https://www.iras.gov.sg/images/default-source/illustrations-png/paying-stamp-duty.png?sfvrsn=42cc5d0d_3)\\
**Loss Carry-Back Relief** \\
\\
Companies may carry back unutilised capital allowances and trade losses arising in a Year of Assessment (YA) to reduce the amount of taxes payable in the immediate preceding YA.](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/corporate-income-tax/income-deductions-for-companies/claiming-reliefs/loss-carry-back-relief)
