The current GST rate in Singapore is 9%. GST-registered businesses are required to charge and account for GST at 9% on all sales of goods and services in Singapore unless the sale can be [zero-rated](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/goods-services-tax-(gst)/charging-gst-(output-tax)/when-to-charge-0-gst-(zero-rate) "zero-rated ") or [exempted](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/goods-services-tax-(gst)/charging-gst-(output-tax)/when-is-gst-not-charged/supplies-exempt-from-gst "exempted ") under the GST law.

## Historical GST rates

GST was introduced in Singapore on 1 Apr 1994.

| Year | GST Rate |
| --- | --- |
| 1 Apr 1994 to 31 Dec 2002 | 3% |
| 1 Jan 2003 to 31 Dec 2003 | 4% |
| 1 Jan 2004 to 30 Jun 2007 | 5% |
| 1 Jul 2007 to 31 Dec 2022 | 7% |
| 1 Jan 2023 to 31 Dec 2023 | 8% |
