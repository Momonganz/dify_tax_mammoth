# What is taxable, what is not

Generally, income earned in or derived from Singapore is chargeable to income tax, while [overseas income received in Singapore](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/individual-income-tax/basics-of-individual-income-tax/what-is-taxable-what-is-not/income-received-from-overseas "overseas income received in Singapore") is
not taxable, except in some circumstances.
